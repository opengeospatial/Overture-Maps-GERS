[[req-gers-datetime]]

[width="90%",cols="2,6"]
|===
^|*Requirement 1* | */req/core/metadata-datetime*
^| A | Searchable date and time. This may be a time instance or time period. The specification of date and time in any GERS metadata/properties specified in a schema _SHALL_ be specified in UTC.
^| B | Date and Time _SHALL_ be formatted according to https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/html/rfc3339[RFC 3339], section 5.6. RFC 3339 is a profile of the ISO 8601 standard for representation of dates and times using the Gregorian calendar.
|===
