[[req-gers-datetime]]

[requirement]

====
[%metadata]
name:: GERS DateTime specification. 
identifier:: /req/core/metadata-datetime
part:: The specification of date and time in any GERS metadata/properties specified in a schema _SHALL_ be specified in UTC.
part:: Date and Time _SHALL_ be formatted according to https://datatracker.ietf.org/doc/html/rfc3339[RFC 3339], section 5.6. RFC 3339 is a profile of the ISO 8601 standard for representation of dates and times using the Gregorian calendar.
guidance:: This is searchable date and time. This may be a time instance or time period
====
